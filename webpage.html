In 2016 Atlante is celebrating its 100 years! Such a big team!

In Mexican soccer Atlante is a historical club. A team that shows that there is always an opportunity to fight against adversity. Atlante has moved from city to city, playing in 1st and 2nd division, struggling to retain top players, but it has never given up in making their supporters feel proud of the team.

Today Atlante doesn't enjoy its better days. In 2nd division and far away from the real fans in Mexico City, but this is just another opportunity to re-birth.

The first 100 years have been fantastic, but the 100 years that are coming are gonna be even better!
